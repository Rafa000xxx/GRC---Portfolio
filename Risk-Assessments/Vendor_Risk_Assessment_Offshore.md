### Risk Assessment: Offshore Support Vendor (GlobalOps Ltd)
**Risk:** Data Leakage via Third-Party Support.
**Threat:** Support agents may have excessive permissions to customer databases.
**Current Control:** Password-only access (Inadequate).
**Residual Risk:** HIGH.
**Recommendation:** Implement **Just-In-Time (JIT)** access provisioning to limit database access to the duration of a support ticket.
